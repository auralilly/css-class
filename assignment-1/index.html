About Citizen Soldier

This is why i realy admire this band. Citizen Soldier is an American rock band founded in 2016, known for using music to raise awareness about mental health issues including depression, anxiety, PTSD, addiction, and suicide prevention. Led by vocalist Jake Segura, the band blends post-hardcore, alternative rock, and cinematic sounds to create deeply personal and relatable songs. Their lyrics often speak directly to listeners who feel misunderstood or isolated.
